About us

Overview

Pagistnation is a budding start-up catering to curbing the latent illiteracy. Focused on transitioning the incompetent and unsure world of photostat study material from the book depots to bringing the same study material directly from teachers and publishers to students.

Thereby providing you the currently non-existing guarantee of authenticity, ease of access and not to mention the assurance of knowing where to look to look for study material.

Our vision

  • Never be limited, yet not overwhelmed while learning.
  • Boundaries fall when you grow.
  • Study anytime, anywhere.
  • Spread your knowledge with us, while we handle the unrelated but important part.
  • Teach and Learn the way that suits.
