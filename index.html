About us

Overview

We are an Ed-Tech start-up focusing on curbing the latent illiteracy. Focused on transitioning the incompetent and unsure world of photostat study material from the book depots to bringing the same study material directly from teachers and publishers to students. Thereby providing the non-existing guarantee of authenticity, ease of access and not to mention the assurance of knowing where to look to look for study material.

Our vision

  • Expanding your boundaries
  • Reusability of data irrespective of time
  • Keep track of time students spend on your notes
  • Easier management and availability
  • Flexibility To teach in the way you want and students learn in the pace Students want
